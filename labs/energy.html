5 — Deriving the Energy Model (Symbolic Reasoning)

Step-by-step guide:

1. Write $E_i = E_f$ for the system.
2. Substitute $mgh$ for $U_g$ and $\frac12 mv^2$ for $K$.
3. Assume $v_i = 0$.
4. Solve for $v_f$.
